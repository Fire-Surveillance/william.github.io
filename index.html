What is AI?

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It is a branch of computer science that deals with the development of intelligent machines and systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation.

AI Applications

Machine Learning

Systems that can learn and improve from experience without explicit programming.

Computer Vision

AI systems that can understand and analyze visual information from the world.

Natural Language Processing

AI that can understand, interpret, and generate human language.
